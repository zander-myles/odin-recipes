Delicious French Fries Recipe

Everyone loves french fries! Here, you'll learn how to make the best french fries in the world!

These are the best french fries you'll ever taste! Not only are they delicious, they are also quick and easy to make!

What you'll need:

  • Potatoes
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Deep frying oil

Steps:

  1. Peel the potatoes.
  2. Wash the potatoes.
  3. Cut the potatoes into french fries.
  4. Soak the naked french fries in water for 30 minutes.
  5. Drain the naked french fries and dry them with a clean cloth towel.
  6. Season the naked french fries with salt and pepper.
  7. Pour oil into a deep pot.
  8. Heat the oil.
  9. Parfry the french fries in the hot oil for 5 minutes.
  10. Drain, dry and let the parfried potatoes cool for 10 minutes.
  11. Finish frying the french fries for another 10 minutes, or until golden brown.
  12. Pat dry the excess oil, lightly season with salt and serve!
